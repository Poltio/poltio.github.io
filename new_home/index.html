New Triggers / Experience Designs

New designs for trigger widget areas and experience

Banner Experience

Product Finder with Banner Experience

On the product list page, we have integrated a Product Finder tool, designed to help users easily navigate and discover products based on their preferences. This tool is prominently featured within a banner at the top of the page, providing a seamless experience that guides users through their search.

Banner Trigger

Product Finder Trigger Button on Banner

On the product list page, we have a Product Finder feature integrated into the banner. The banner includes a prominent trigger button that users can click to activate the product finder. When clicked, this button opens up a more detailed search experience, allowing users to filter and customize their product search based on their preferences, such as category, price, size, and more.

Area with Custom Logo

Custom Trigger Area for Product Finder

On the product list page, we have a fixed area that features a custom logo and a brief description, designed specifically to trigger the Product Finder. This section remains in a consistent position on the page, providing users with easy access to the product finder tool, no matter where they are on the page.

Informational Area Trigger

Informational Area for Triggering the Product Finder

We have a dedicated informational area near the trigger for the product finder that functions similarly to a disclaimer. While it isn't a formal disclaimer, this section provides users with important context or guidance before they use the product finder. It appears near the trigger and helps set expectations about how the tool works or any important considerations users should keep in mind.

Fixed Button with Custom Logo and Text

Fixed Button with Custom Trigger for Product Finder

On the home page, we have a fixed button that remains visible as users scroll through the page. This button is designed to trigger the product finder tool. It features a custom logo and personalized trigger text, making it both visually appealing and easy to understand.
