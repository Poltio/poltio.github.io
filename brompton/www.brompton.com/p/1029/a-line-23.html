The Brompton Fold

Invented by our founder, Andrew Ritchie, in 1975, expertly engineered to transform the bike into a small, locked package a little larger than its wheels—all in under 20-seconds. Nothing folds like a Brompton folds.

All the essentials

A Window Grey & Turkish Green steel frame. The original 3-part fold. Three easy-to-use hub gears. A natural sit-up and enjoy the ride position with a riser handlebar. And Genuine Brompton parts and fittings compatibility, so you can add as you go.

As easy as 1,2,3

Ready to roll? The A Line hub gears let you shift when stationary, so setting off is easy. Simply nudge the single lever up or down. The three gears are sealed within the middle part of the rear wheel. Protected from water, grit and impact, they enjoy a long life with minimum maintenance.

Which Brompton is right for me?

Your Brompton bike can come in many combinations. Some like colour are easy to choose, but other options such as the handlebar type and seatpost type are more difficult to get right. Let us help you out!

A Line - 3-speed

Set out for every ride in style with Brompton's stunning A Line model. With a brilliant white finish that represents the pure enjoyment experienced from riding such an ergonomic bike, you can unlock a world of opportunity at the push of a pedal.

This sensational folding bike offers a unique ride thanks to the lightweight, hand-crafted frame which improves speed and agility. Also including 3-hub gears and strong brakes, you can glide, race, and roll at your desired pace. The A Line gear technology is unique as it lets you shift down when stationary making for a much smoother start to any journey. Whether you are exploring a characterful city or a rugged outdoor area, a joyful ride is guaranteed.

The stunning white silhouette is enhanced by rich steel and soulful black accents making for a stylish bike. Furthermore, the mid-handlebar, which offers improved posture for the rider, makes for an even more comfortable riding experience. All of this is perfectly wrapped up in a light and compact frame, which weighs just 11.5kgs. This makes it simple to get around and carry your bike when converted to its folded design which is the same size as a small suitcase. Simple to carry, ease, easy to store, and a pleasure to ride; this bike has it all.

With an upright position guaranteed, you can ride with your body relaxed and mind close to the clouds for a carefree experience.. Brompton offers more than just a bike; it provides a new way of living.

HOME DELIVERY

Enjoy free delivery on all orders over £50. £4.95 delivery fee for any orders under £50. We aim to dispatch your order within 2 working days; sometimes this can be longer during busy periods.
