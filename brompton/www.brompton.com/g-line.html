G Line at a glance

G Line is the first ever Brompton with the ride feel of a full-size bike. Brand-new geometry, 20" wheels, game-changing comfort and capability.

Available in 3 different sizes for the best ride experience.

Game-changing comfort

Nothing feels like G Line – the entirely new frame, larger 20” wheels, our widest ever handlebars and tyres. 

Everything is thoughtfully engineered to give the balanced feel of a full-size bike and all-day comfort that’s nothing short of joyous. You’ll feel the difference on every ride.

Big on ride feel, compact in size

We created G Line to be the best one-bike solution for the city and beyond, and then we made it a Brompton. 

It rides like a full-sized bike, yet it transforms into a compact package that stores and travels easily. So you can go anywhere.

The most capable Brompton ever

Rigorously tested over 250,000 km, first to meet industry standards, then to meet ours, G Line goes where others can’t. Now, you can confidently tackle everything from dusty single track to rain-soaked cobbles. Built tough with disc brake stopping power, this bike does it all with ease.

A closer look

Larger 20" wheels

Big enough for any adventure yet compact enough to fit inside the fold. The G Line’s solidly built 20” wheelset comes equipped with custom-made puncture-resistant tyres.

And if you’re looking for even more off-road performance and protection, they are tubeless-ready whenever you are.

Hydraulic disc brakes

Super dependable stopping power. The first ever Brompton to come equipped with disc brakes for all terrain, all-weather riding.

Uphill, downhill, rough, smooth, with luggage, in the rain, you can do it all on G Line.

Shimano Alfine Hub Gear

A solidly built 8-gear system that covers all grounds. Super simple to use and brilliantly low maintenance. There’s just one trigger on your handlebars. And the gears are tucked away inside your rear wheel hub - protected from water, mud, and impact.

Wide platform pedals

The grippiest, most comfortable Brompton pedal set. For riding on all kinds of ground.

And if you want to make your folded bike even more compact, you can remove the left-hand pedal with a quick squeeze and pull, then stow it securely behind your handlebar.

Wide handlebars
Stay in control. With the custom-designed handlebars, G Line gives you exceptional stability – from leverage on uphills and rough ground to comfort on longer rides. Expertly engineered to fit inside the fold.
Ergonomic grips

Every touchpoint between you and the bike is designed with comfort and control in mind.

The all-new Brompton Ergonomic Grips are developed and tested by us for G Line, so you have the right cushioning and support exactly where you need it.

Brompton Superlight Saddle

Brompton-designed saddles enhance your ride and more. The Superlight Saddle carbon base quietens vibrations and reduces pressures.

The carry handle under the nose makes lifting and rolling your folded bike even easier. And when it comes to adding Brompton Luggage or lights, the mount and loops are all in place.

Brompton engineering

A fold designed for life

From a go-anywhere bike to a compact package in under 20 seconds. It stores easily. Tucks in car boots and campers and even one-person tents.

There is no need to lock and leave your G Line – it fits into your life indoors and out.

Moving with this folded bike is easy

Fold halfway. Steer with the saddle or tow with your handlebar. G Line stays locked together and glides like a wheelie suitcase. Breeze through train stations, along plazas and into buildings. Nothing folds like a Brompton folds.

Carry everything you need

Brompton Bags sit securely on the front of your bike with one quick click. The unique mounting system places the weight on your frame instead of the handlebars. So when carrying loads, your ride remains brilliantly stable.

Built tough

Built on fifty years of expertise, Brompton bikes are made for real life and a long life. G Line’s production is a labour of love, with each steel frame handmade and quality assured in our London Factory.

With our 7-year frame warranty, you can ride with peace of mind.

G Line sizes

G Line comes in three easy-to-choose sizes based on your height. Small, medium or large. The single-size frame has varying stem and seatpost heights, developed and tested with a world-leading bike fit expert for the best ride experience.

For more information on how to choose, watch our useful video guide.

FAQs

-

What terrain can I ride G Line on?

It’s the most capable Brompton yet, with the ability to deliver on all sorts of surfaces,  including: gravel paths, tow paths, asphalt and well maintained off-road routes. With the feel of a full-size bike.

How many gears does G Line have?

The G line has 8 gears with a 307% gear ratio/range. It has a Shimano Alfine 8-speed hub gear.  

What is the warranty of G Line?

The frame comes with a 7-year-warranty once registered.

What is the reach of each size?
  • The small has a reach of 477mm 
  • The medium has a reach of 483mm  
  • The large has a reach of 504mm 
How do I park my G Line?

The G Line supports itself when in park mode. Simply flip the rear wheel under and see the magic.

Does G Line luggage fit on my C, P, T Line Front Carrier Block?

Yes, all our bags use the same mounting system on the front of the bike.

Does it come with Dynamo?

No.
