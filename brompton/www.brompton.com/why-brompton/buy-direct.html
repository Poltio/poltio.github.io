    New to Brompton?

    For over 50 years, we’ve brought freedom and happiness into the lives of people all around the world with our folding bikes. And we’re not hitting the brakes any time soon.

    Nobody knows Brompton like we do and there’s never been a better way to get your hands on one than direct from us. Here’s why.

    28-day guarantee

    If, after a few rides, your Brompton doesn’t quite fit into your life, return the bike in good condition within 28 days for a full refund.

    Read more

    Fast and convenient delivery

    With most bikes shipped for free within 48 hours, you can get your Brompton sent to your home, workplace or even your granny’s house. Or you can take advantage of our Click & Collect service at your local Brompton dealer.
