    Brompton Renewed

    Display, demo and gently used Brompton bikes - looking for their next adventure.

    Up to 20% off.

    Shop now

    Tested, graded, and Brompton certified.

    Grade 1
    Fully serviced and ready to ride. No signs of use.
    Grade 2
    Fully serviced and ready to ride. Up to 4 visible marks, no bigger than 2cm each.
    Grade 3
    Fully serviced and ready to ride. Over 4 visible marks, one or more in excess of 2cm each.

    Ready to get back on the road

    You could say some of these bikes have been around the block while others have been sat in the comfort of our showroom. We have graded them (in a non judgemental way) accordingly - with savings up to 20% off. Don't fret - you'll still feel like you're flying on a magic carpet. Any imperfections of Brompton Renewed bikes are purely cosmetic and won't affect your ride.

    Peace of mind guaranteed

    Every Brompton Renewed bike is given a detailed 27-point safety check and is fully serviced by our expert technicians. We always use genuine Brompton parts. Only once the bike passes with flying colours does it receive our 7-year registered frame warranty.

    Delivered to your door

    If you live within the UK* we'll deliver your bike directly to you. Free of charge.

    *excluding the Channel Islands and Isle of Man

    Reducing our carbon footprint

    We know getting any bike on the road is good for the planet. But that's only part of the equation. Our bikes empower the community of Brompton owners to reduce their own footprint with a sustainable transport solution. Building our bikes creates its own footprint - and we're keen to reduce waste. 96% of our global operational energy is sourced via renewable energy contracts, supporting our commitment to Business Ambition for 1.5°C.
