Brompton Electric

The power to take you anywhere.

Shop Electric Bikes

The Brompton Electric range offers compact, folding e-bikes designed for city living and beyond. Combining Brompton’s iconic folding design with the all-new Brompton e-Motiq system, these bikes make riding easier, faster, and even more fun.

So, whether you’re looking for a super light, compact bike for inner-city life, or a versatile, go-anywhere bike for adventures further afield, there’s a model to suit you.

All-new Brompton Electric

Power meets portability in the new Brompton Electric range.

Expertly engineered to go even further with the innovative e-Motiq system. Made by Brompton for Brompton. The custom-designed hardware and software perform together seamlessly to create the best electric experience in the city and beyond.

Powered by the Brompton e-Motiq system

Made by Brompton for Brompton. The custom-designed hardware and software perform together seamlessly to create the best electric experience in the city and beyond.

Smile-inducing smoothness

The all-new Brompton Electric has been fine-tuned with a new custom-built 250W rear hub motor to give exceptional traction and superb control. Add to that the new Start Assist Mode and every journey feels so easy.

Compact power

With the 345 Wh battery, you can explore up to 90 km in one go. And when it comes to charging, it’s super easy. Remove the battery pack with one quick click and plug in anywhere.

Quietly intelligent

From the new and intuitive handlebar control to our first-ever fully synchronised app, with this bike everything and everywhere connects. Over the first 100 km of riding, your Brompton Electric starts to build a good understanding of your riding style so it can make smart range predictions. Every 25 m you ride it personalises even further.

Brompton engineering

Invention, imagination and innovation have been at the heart of Brompton since 1975, transforming the way people move in and around cities across the world.

Iconic folding design

A bike that transforms from a go-anywhere vehicle to a small, locked package in under 20 seconds.

Everything folds, even the pedal.

Super portable

Moving folded has never been this easy. Lift the handlebar for Shopper Mode. Drop the seatpost for Suitcase Mode. Push. Pull. Steer. Glide. That’s SuperRoll.

Add the Roller Frame and you unlock SuperRoll+. It shifts the centre of gravity, so your bike feels lighter. Four wheels on the base mean even faster, smoother, more stable rolling.

Built to last

Brompton bikes are made for real life and a long life. Each bike is hand built, rigorously tested and quality assured in our London Factory. We are proud of what we do, that’s why every Brompton is marked by its maker.

Serious about safety

Designed with the same systems engineering approach found in the automotive and aerospace industries, the Brompton Electric sets the benchmark for quality and safety.

Our bikes are independently certified to the UL2849 standard, the most comprehensive e-bike electrical test out there.

Brompton Luggage System

You never know where the day might take you. Or what you need to carry. Brompton Bags give you flexibility to load up when you need. Food shopping? Overnight stay? Padel after work?

Our range of Electric bags have space for your battery and more. The unique mounting system places the weight on your frame instead of the handlebars. So, when carrying loads, your ride remains brilliantly stable.
