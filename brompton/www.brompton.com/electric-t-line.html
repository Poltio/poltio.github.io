    Electric T Line

    Ultralight. Titanium. Electric.

    In pursuit of lightweight performance

    The Electric T Line was conceived to lift the Brompton feeling to a new plane, engineered with a lightness unlike anything you’ve experienced – at least, not yet. We’ve taken the best of the Brompton experience and amplified it with the power of titanium, then electrified it.

    With an all-titanium frame that’s ultralight and as strong as steel, and super responsive carbon components, you feel the wow with every ride.

    Add to this the smoother, faster folding mechanisms and experience freedom in the city like never before.

    Brompton e-Motiq System

    With the all-new e-system, you get the best electric system. Meticulously engineered by Brompton for you.

    With up to 90 km of range and 3 assistance modes, this new motor and battery system is designed to help you go further without breaking a sweat.

    Tested for durability, serious about safety

    Designed with the same systems engineering approach found in the automotive and aerospace industries, the Brompton Electric sets the benchmark for quality and safety.

    Our bikes are independently certified to the UL2849 standard, the most comprehensive e-bike electrical test out there.

    Quietly intelligent

    From the new and intuitive handlebar control to our first-ever fully synchronised app, with this bike everything and everywhere connects. Over the first 100 km of riding, your Brompton Electric starts to build a good understanding of your riding style so it can make smart range predictions. Every 6 km you ride it personalises even further.

    Handbuilt in London

    Built on fifty years of expertise, Brompton bikes are made for real life and a long life. Each frame is handmade and quality assured in our London Factory.

    Enhance your ride

    Brompton Luggage System

    You never know where the day might take you. Or what you need to carry. Brompton Bags give you flexibility to load up when you need. Food shopping? Overnight stay? Padel after work?

    Our range of Electric bags have space for your battery and more. The unique mounting system places the weight on your frame instead of the handlebars. So, when carrying loads, your ride remains brilliantly stable.

    Best portability ever

    Moving folded has never been this easy. Lift the handlebar for Shopper Mode. Drop the seatpost for Suitcase Mode. Push. Pull. Steer. Glide. That’s SuperRoll.

    Add the Roller Frame and you unlock SuperRoll+. It shifts the centre of gravity, so your bike feels lighter. Four wheels on the base mean even faster, smoother, more stable rolling.

    Electric T Line FAQs

    .

    Can I take my Brompton Electric bike on public transport?

    Brompton Electric bikes can be taken on most forms of public transport including Transport for London services. Check with the local operator if in doubt.

    How far can I ride the Electric T Line on a single charge?

    Depending on assist level, terrain, and rider weight, the range is up to 90km per charge.

    How does the Brompton Electric T Line compared to the Electric P Line?

    The main difference is frame material: the Electric T Line has a full titanium frame, while the P Line has only a titanium rear. The T Line is also nearly 2kg lighter, with more premium components and an optional single-speed version for maximum weight savings.

    Can the Electric T Line be ridden without the battery or with assistance turned off?

    Yes, the Electric T Line can be ridden without battery assistance like a regular Brompton.

    Can I test ride or finance a Brompton Electric T Line?

    Test rides can be booked at your local Brompton dealer. Find out more about the many flexible ways to pay for your new Brompton Electric T Line.
