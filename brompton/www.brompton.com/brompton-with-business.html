    Brompton with Business

    From flexible leasing to fleet ownership, Brompton offers smarter mobility solutions for modern businesses moving through the city.

    Enquire Now

    Why Brompton?

    Here are some ways Brompton Bicycle can help your business

    Cost savings

    Bicycle usage can significantly reduce travel expenses compared to taxis, company vehicles, or mileage reimbursements. 

    Urban efficiency

    Perfect for short trips between offices, train stations, or client sites. With no need to find parking, simply fold your Brompton and take it with you.  

    Employee health & wellbeing

    Cycling promotes physical activity and mental well-being. On average, healthier employees have an average of 1.5 fewer sick days per year, equal to £83M for the UK economy.  

    Sustainable transport

    Cycling offers a sustainable transport solution that supports corporate environmental goals to reduce carbon footprint and work toward net-zero targets.

    In Europe, a car emits 271g CO2 per passenger-kilometre. Riding your bike is around 21g CO2 per kilometre.

     What do we offer?

    Bespoke Bike Fleets

    Brompton can provide bespoke bike fleets, personalised to your company. Bespoke fleets come in your preferred bike model and carry your company branding.

    Additional customisation levels include logo decals, choice of frame colours, as well as parts & accessories. 

    Enquire now

    Custom Bike Subscription

    Through our subscription model, we provide a convenient pool of bikes for your employees without the capital outlay. Ideal for equipping employees with a Brompton Bicycle they can use at their leisure, or for gifting purposes. 

    It is also possible to upgrade to a managed system, where you can access the fleet of bikes via our app, allowing employees or guests to hire and return at will. Subscription includes full servicing, theft cover and liability insurance. 

    Enquire now

    Brompton lockers

    Installing a Brompton bike locker offers a compact, secure, and sustainable way that can support employee commuting.
