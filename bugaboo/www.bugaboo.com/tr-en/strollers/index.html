FAQ’s

Bugaboo offers several types of strollers to suit your family's size and needs, including: Comfort strollers that offer a smooth ride on all terrains; compact strollers that are small and lightweight for travel and city life; double strollers with two seats or bassinets for siblings to ride side by side.
When choosing a Bugaboo stroller, it's important to consider your lifestyle and needs. Some of the features you might want to consider are: the age of your child, terrain, size and weight, safety features and additional features. It's always a good idea to go for a test drive in person to get a feel for the stroller before making a final decision.
Yes, our strollers are designed to be easy to fold and store. Many of our strollers can be folded with one hand, including the Bugaboo Butterfly and Bugaboo Fox 5. The folded stroller can also stand on its own, making it easy to store in small spaces like a closet or a hallway.
The price range for Bugaboo strollers can vary depending on your family needs, the model you choose and whether you've picked any additional accessories or fabric options. Head over to each product page to view the latest price options.
Many of our strollers can be used with infant car seats including the Bugaboo Turtle Air by Nuna, letting you use the stroller as a travel system. For a safe and easy connection, you will need a Bugaboo car seat adapter (sold separately). You can check what car seats are compatible with a Bugaboo stroller on the stroller's description page.
Yes, our Bugaboo comfort strollers can handle all types of terrain, including gravel, cobblestones, sand and grass. They feature larger wheels and advanced suspension to help you navigate even the most bumpy paths.
All Bugaboo strollers include a standard two-year warranty for all parts and servicing. Enjoy an additional two years on top of that by registering your product on Bugaboo.com.
Yes, many of our strollers are suitable for newborns. Some Bugaboo strollers (such as the Bugaboo Fox 5) come with a bassinet for your baby, and others can be used with a car seat adapter and a compatible infant car seat (such as the Bugaboo Turtle Air by Nuna). We suggest you double check the weight and age limits of the specific stroller to make sure it meets your baby's needs.
The weight limit for Bugaboo strollers varies depending on your model and region. Typically, the weight limit is 10 kg/22 lbs in the bassinet and 22 kg/50 lbs in the seat. We suggest you double check the weight and age limits of the specific stroller to make sure it meets your child's needs.
