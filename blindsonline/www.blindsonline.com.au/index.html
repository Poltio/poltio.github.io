Made to Measure Blinds

At Tuiss Blinds Online, we offer the largest range of made to measure blinds and curtains in Australia. With speedy delivery, free samples, and incredible value for money, update your home for less today. Whether you're looking for the perfect window dressing for your bedroom window, children's room window, bay window or french and patio door, we have you covered.

Find Your Perfect Window Dressing

Browse our Shutter, Timber and Aluminium Venetian Blinds that combine elegance and practicality, with robust slats made from real wood, faux wood or aluminium. Our Roller Blinds, Vertical Blinds and Roman Blinds offer gorgeous style too, with a plethora of shades and fabrics to peruse alongside your choice of lining. Featuring patterns from William Morris in collaboration with the V&A, Emma Bridgewater, Harlequin, Clarissa Hulse and more brands, we have window blinds to go in every space.

There's also Honeycomb Blinds that help you improve energy efficiency all year round with a honeycomb fabric, Total Blockout Blinds that stop the light in its tracks, as well as Fly Screen Roller Blinds that help keep pests at bay year round.

Innovations In Shading

For extra functionality, our electric upgrade offers innovative shading via the touch of our Tuiss SmartView app, allowing you to set it and forget it from anywhere in your home. You can also enjoy easier installation with our no drill upgrades, replacing brackets with a click or twist mechanism. For luxurious outdoor shading that adds instant appeal to your garden and patio area, look no further than our extensive range of Outdoor Blinds, from Pivot Arm Awnings to Patio and Wire Guide Blinds.

Shop Blinds By Colour

Explore a fantastic spectrum of colours and find a shade that pairs harmoniously with your interior scheme. From vibrant hues to neutral tones, including contemporary greys, tranquil blues, and lively greens, create a stunning statement in almost any window.

Shop Blinds By Room

Create a captivating focal point in each room of your home with made to measure blinds to suit every need. Whether you're looking for waterproof bathroom blinds and kitchen blinds, textured living room blinds, blackout bedroom blinds or door blinds that offer a perfect fit, our window coverings blend style and functionality seamlessly in every space.
